ABOUT

What is Healthcare Data Hub?

Healthcare Data Hub was created in 2021 to solve one problem in healthcare: modern management of patient information. You shouldn't have to fill out paper forms on a clipboard, only to have an office person in the clinic hand-enter that information into a computer system. You also shouldn't have to continually provide the same information over and over again, to different doctors, specialists, and hospitals. Our service is always free for patients. In a nutshell, you fill out the same information about your health that you'd give to a doctor, and then any doctor you authorize can get instant access to your data if you authorize them to access it. Nothing is shared without your permission. You can see who has access to your information, and you can change it at any time.

How does it work?

When you sign-up for Healthcare Data Hub, you will be presented with the same type of medical information forms that your doctor's office asks for. You fill these forms out at your own convenience. Then, when you visit any participating medical provider, they will send you a request to share this information with them. You receive the request electronically via either email or our mobile app, and with the press of one button you can authorize your doctor to have your information. Healthcare Data Hub takes care of the rest for you and you can rest assured that your doctor knows everything they need to know about your medical history so that they can provide you with the best care possible.
